About Me

I am Ajay Prasath Manoharan, a Data Scientist specializing in fraud detection and prevention at Truist Financial Corp. My expertise lies in leveraging machine learning, data modeling, and advanced analytics to identify fraud patterns and reduce operational risks. Through collaboration with key stakeholders and cross-functional teams, I have developed fraud prevention strategies that have resulted in significant reductions in card fraud and operational costs, saving millions in potential fraud losses.

With skills in Python, SAS, Tableau, and SQL, I lead projects that focus on fraud intelligence, anomaly detection, and real-time monitoring, ensuring financial safety for both businesses and consumers. I am passionate about using data science to solve complex fraud challenges, constantly improving model accuracy while minimizing false positives and enhancing customer experience.
